Live Group Tracking

A live group tracking lets you share your location during an activity.

Anyone can join a group using a link or a QR code, without necessarily needing a Trailence account or the application installed.

Each member’s location is regularly recorded and shared with the other group members, so you can easily find each other if needed.
Of course, location sharing only works when a network connection is available. However, you can disable your network during your activity and only turn it on when necessary.

A group is automatically deleted if it is more than one week old.
