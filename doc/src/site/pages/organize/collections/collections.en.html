Collections

Collections allow you to organize your trails into folders.
For example:

  • My Trails → the trails you've recorded
  • To Do → trails you want to do
  • 🌴Trips → trails from your travels
  • ...

Each collection lets you view a map with all its trails, browse them, sort, filter, search...

To organize trails within the same collection, you can use tags.

My Trails

The "My Trails" collection is created automatically when you create your account.

  • It always appears first,
  • When you record a track with your GPS, the track is automatically saved in this collection (but you can then move or copy them)
  • It cannot be deleted (because it's where your tracks go by default), but you can rename it.

All collections

The "All collections" menu gives the same view by grouping all trails from your collections.

Copy / Move

  • The "Copy to..." menu allows you to copy one or more trails into another collection
  • The "Move to..." menu allows you to move one or more trails into another collection
