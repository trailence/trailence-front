Tags

Tags let you organize trails within a collection by assigning labels to your trails.
For example, you can create tags:

  • With the names of people who were with you → makes it easy to find trails with a person and share those trails with them
  • "bivouac" → find all your bivouac trails
  • "urban" → city visit routes
  • "multi-day" → trails over several days
  • etc...

A trail can have multiple tags.
